East Coast Park

Best public park for everything and anything. (Yelp, 2014)

Activities

1. Beach day

Visit the beaches at East Coast Park on warm and sunny days!
Invite friends and families to build sandcastles, catch an amazing sunset and more!

2. Water activities

East Coast Park is lined up with a wide variety of water sports:

Water Ski

For thrill seekers! Water skiing at East Coast Park is a good place for beginners too!
Instructors will be there to aid and guide do not fret

Aloha Sea Sports Centre

Aloha Sea Sports provides different water sports experience for those who love the sea
Some of these activities include: Windsurfing, Windfoiling, Stand Up Paddling and Laser Sailing
Interested? Click here for rates!

3. Cycling

East Coast park provides a long cycling trail that leads to exciting attractions such as Gardens by The Bays, Changi Airport and more!

For dinosaur lovers, take a ride along the newly built dinosaur trail to the airport!
